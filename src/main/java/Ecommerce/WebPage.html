Health Benefits of Wood-Pressed Oils

Higher Nutrient Retention

The gentle pressing at low temperatures ensures that essential vitamins, enzymes, and nutrients are not destroyed by heat, making these oils richer in nutritional content.

Rich in Antioxidants

These oils maintain high levels of antioxidants which help in combating oxidative stress and reducing the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and cancer.

Free from Harmful Additives

Since there is no use of chemicals or high heat in the extraction process, wood-pressed oils do not contain any synthetic additives, preservatives, or trans fats.

Better Flavor and Aroma

The natural flavors and aromas of the seeds or nuts are preserved in the oil, enhancing the taste and sensory experience of meals.

Healthy Fats

Wood-pressed oils provide essential fatty acids, including omega-3 and omega-6, which are crucial for maintaining heart health, supporting brain function, and promoting healthy skin.

Improved Heart Health

These oils generally contain higher levels of monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats and lower levels of unhealthy saturated fats, contributing to better cardiovascular health.

Supports Weight Management

The healthier fat profile can help in managing weight effectively when used as part of a balanced diet.

Anti-inflammatory Properties

The natural properties of wood-pressed oils include anti-inflammatory effects, which can help reduce inflammation in the body, benefiting conditions such as arthritis.

Enhances Skin Health

The high nutrient and antioxidant content can have a positive effect on skin, enhancing its elasticity and hydration, and preventing premature aging.

Digestive Health Benefits

These oils are easier on the stomach compared to heavily processed oils, helping in better digestion and overall gut health.

Our Process: From Seed to Bottle

Sourcing Ingredients

We carefully select the finest seeds and nuts for our oils, ensuring that they are of the highest quality and purity. Our ingredients are sourced from local farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.

Traditional Wood Pressing Technique

Our oils are extracted using a traditional wood pressing technique that has been passed down through generations. This method involves crushing the seeds or nuts with a wooden press to extract the oil, preserving its natural flavors and nutrients.

Natural Filtration and Settling

After extraction, the oil is allowed to naturally settle and filter, removing any impurities or sediments. This process ensures that the oil is pure and free from contaminants, maintaining its quality and freshness.

Bottling and Packaging

Once the oil has been extracted and filtered, it is carefully bottled and packaged to preserve its freshness and flavor. Our bottles are designed to protect the oil from light and air, ensuring that it remains pure and potent until it reaches your kitchen.

Quality Assurance

Before our oils are ready for sale, they undergo rigorous quality assurance checks to ensure that they meet our high standards of purity and freshness. We take pride in delivering the finest wood pressed oils to our customers, with a commitment to quality and excellence.

Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Practices

At Wooden Oils, we are dedicated to sustainability and eco-friendly practices.
