WHY WOOD PRESSED OILS..?

Wood pressed oils are crafted using traditional techniques that preserve the natural flavors, nutrients, and purity of the oil. This method, also known as cold pressing, avoids the use of high temperatures and harsh chemicals, ensuring that the oil retains its essential fatty acids, antioxidants, and vitamins. Unlike refined oils, wood pressed oils offer a healthier alternative, with more complex flavors and a higher nutritional profile. They are environmentally friendly as well, requiring less energy and producing fewer byproducts. Choose wood pressed oils for a wholesome, eco-friendly choice that supports both your health and the planet.

Our Process: From Seed to Bottle

Sourcing Ingredients

We carefully select the finest seeds and nuts for our oils, ensuring that they are of the highest quality and purity. Our ingredients are sourced from local farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.

Traditional Wood Pressing Technique

Our oils are extracted using a traditional wood pressing technique that has been passed down through generations. This method involves crushing the seeds or nuts with a wooden press to extract the oil, preserving its natural flavors and nutrients.

Natural Filtration and Settling

After extraction, the oil is allowed to naturally settle and filter, removing any impurities or sediments. This process ensures that the oil is pure and free from contaminants, maintaining its quality and freshness.

Bottling and Packaging

Once the oil has been extracted and filtered, it is carefully bottled and packaged to preserve its freshness and flavor. Our bottles are designed to protect the oil from light and air, ensuring that it remains pure and potent until it reaches your kitchen.

Quality Assurance

Before our oils are ready for sale, they undergo rigorous quality assurance checks to ensure that they meet our high standards of purity and freshness. We take pride in delivering the finest wood pressed oils to our customers, with a commitment to quality and excellence.

Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Practices

At Wooden Oils, we are dedicated to sustainability and eco-friendly practices. We work closely with local farmers and suppliers to support ethical sourcing and production methods that minimize our environmental impact. By choosing our wood pressed oils, you are making a conscious choice to support a healthier planet and a more sustainable future.

Health Benefits

Wood pressed oils offer a range of health benefits, including essential fatty acids, antioxidants, and vitamins that are essential for overall well-being. These oils are rich in nutrients and free from harmful chemicals, making them a healthier alternative to refined oils. Incorporating wood pressed oils into your diet can help support heart health, improve digestion, and boost your immune system, providing you with a natural source of energy and vitality.
